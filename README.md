🧸 Toy Store Sales & Inventory Analysis
## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing sales, product, store, and inventory data for a toy store chain to uncover actionable business insights. The goal is to help stakeholders understand performance trends, optimize inventory, and improve store-level decision-making using SQL and Power BI.



## 🎯 Business Objective
The toy store’s data was scattered across multiple spreadsheets with inconsistent formats, making it difficult to:

- Track sales trends
- Identify top and underperforming products and stores
- Monitor inventory health

This project consolidates the data and delivers a single source of truth through interactive dashboards and structured analysis.



## 📊 Datasets Used

- **Sales** – Transaction-level sales data
- **Products** – Product details including category and pricing
- **Stores** – Store information, location, and performance
- **Inventory** – Stock levels by product and store



## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

- **SQL** – Data cleaning, joins, aggregations, KPI calculations
- **Power BI** – Data modeling, DAX measures, interactive dashboards
- **Excel** – Initial data exploration and validation



## 🔍 Key Insights

- Sales declined by **6.97% in 2023 compared to 2022**, mainly during non-festive months
- Toys category contributed **36% of total sales**, followed by Art & Crafts (20%)
- **Downtown stores generated 57% of total revenue**, outperforming residential locations
- Toys Ciudad de Mexico 2 was the top-performing store, while Toys La Paz 1 showed the lowest performance
- **Inventory turnover (~18.5)** indicates healthy stock movement overall



## 💡 Business Recommendations

- Run targeted promotions during non-festive periods
- Focus on high-performing toy categories to increase profitability
- Clear slow-moving inventory using discounts or bundles
- Analyze low-performing stores and replicate best practices
- Track KPIs monthly using Power BI dashboards



## 📈 Dashboard Highlights

- Sales trend analysis (YoY & monthly)
- Top & bottom store performance
- Product and category contribution
- Inventory turnover and stock efficiency
- Location-based sales analysis



## 🚀 Learnings

- Building scalable data models in Power BI
- Writing optimized SQL queries for business KPIs
- Translating data into actionable insights
- Creating executive-friendly dashboards
